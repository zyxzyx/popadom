About Us

We look forward to welcoming you to a delicious treat of the most authentic Indian food served in Essex. Our chefs have carefully put together our menu with some of the most popular traditional Indian dishes, both for a quick bite or for a full course meal. Our friendly staff, who are also very knowledgeable about our Indian cuisine, will be happy to explain our cuisine and serve you to ensure you have a wonderful experience.
